Before the checklist

Start with the problem, not the tool

The most common mistake small businesses make with AI is starting from "what can this tool do?" instead of "what is costing us the most time every week?" The result is a shiny demo that nobody uses.

This guide flips that around. We've ranked the 7 processes that most often deserve automation first — based on real production systems we've built for restaurants, recruiters, support teams and B2B sales.

"We don't automate for the sake of automation. We automate exactly what's slowing your business down." — and that starts with knowing which process to pick.

For each of the 7 processes below you'll find the same three things, so you can scan and self-diagnose:

Signs it's time

B
What an AI agent does

Typical time saved

Tip: don't try to automate all seven at once. Pick the one or two with the strongest "signs it's time" and the highest weekly hours — that's where your payback is fastest. The ROI calculator on our site can put a number on it.

The checklist
Processes 1–3 of 7
01 Customer inquiries & support routing Saves 40–60%
Signs it's time
  • The same questions get asked over and over
  • Replies are slow at nights / weekends
  • Tickets sit unrouted until someone triages them
What an AI agent does
  • Answers FAQs instantly from your own docs (RAG)
  • Routes complex cases to the right person with context
  • Escalates to a human when it's unsure
02 Document processing & data entry Saves 50–70%
Signs it's time
  • Staff retype data from PDFs, emails or forms
  • Copy-paste errors slip into your systems
  • Invoices / orders pile up waiting to be logged
What an AI agent does
  • Reads documents and extracts the key fields
  • Validates and writes them to your sheet / CRM / DB
  • Flags anything that looks wrong for review
03 CV / candidate screening Up to 10× faster
Signs it's time
  • You receive high volumes of applications
  • Shortlisting eats hours of a hiring manager's week
  • Screening quality varies by who does it
What an AI agent does
  • Scores every CV against your role criteria (0–100)
  • Returns a ranked shortlist with reasoning
  • Logs every decision for a fair audit trail

Each pattern above maps to a real system we've shipped — see neolithai.netlify.app/cases.

The checklist
Processes 4–7 of 7
04 Reviews & reputation management Saves 5–8 h/wk
Signs it's time
  • Reviews on Google / TripAdvisor go unanswered
  • You hear about a bad review days too late
  • Responses are inconsistent in tone
What an AI agent does
  • Monitors review platforms in near real-time
  • Drafts on-brand replies for one-click approval
  • Alerts you instantly on negative sentiment
05 Lead generation & outreach Fully autonomous
Signs it's time
  • Prospecting is manual and stop-start
  • Follow-ups fall through the cracks
  • Your pipeline depends on one person's effort
What an AI agent does
  • Finds & enriches leads, scores them with AI
  • Writes personalised cold emails and sends them
  • Waits, follows up, and reports results to you
06 Reporting & analytics Saves 3–6 h/wk
Signs it's time
  • Someone rebuilds the same report every week
  • Data lives in 3+ disconnected tools
  • Decisions wait on "let me pull the numbers"
What an AI agent does
  • Pulls data from your sources on a schedule
  • Summarises it in plain language with highlights
  • Delivers it to email / Telegram automatically
07 Content & social media 9× output
Signs it's time
  • Posting is irregular because it's manual
  • Re-writing for each platform / language is slow
  • Good ideas never become published content
What an AI agent does
  • Turns one idea into posts for each platform
  • Adapts across languages in one pass
  • Generates matching images and logs everything
